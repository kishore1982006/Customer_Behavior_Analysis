# Customer_Behavior_Analysis
Data Analytics Project Showcasing Customer Behavior Analysis using Python,SQL, and  Power BI.

## End-to-End Data Analytics Project

An end-to-end Data Analytics project focused on understanding customer purchasing behavior using transactional retail data. This project transforms raw customer shopping data into actionable business insights using Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, and Power BI.

---

# Project Overview

Retail businesses generate massive amounts of customer transaction data every day. Understanding customer behavior helps organizations improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, increase revenue, and strengthen customer retention.

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using 3,900+ customer purchase records to identify patterns, customer segments, product trends, and purchasing drivers.

---

# Business Problem Statement

A retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

The company aims to answer:

**"How can customer shopping data be leveraged to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?"**

# Key Business Insights

### Customer Segmentation Matters

Loyal customers contribute significantly more revenue compared to new customers.

### Subscription Programs Drive Engagement

Subscribers demonstrate stronger purchasing behavior and increased retention.

### Discounts Influence Purchase Decisions

Some products heavily rely on discounts for sales generation.

### Demographics Impact Revenue

Age groups and customer profiles affect purchasing patterns.

### Customer Reviews Influence Product Success

Highly rated products generally demonstrate stronger performance.
